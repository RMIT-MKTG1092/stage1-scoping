Quick guide: use publicly available data (Statista, IBISWorld, ABS). If you cannot find an exact figure, show your working and cite the source.

  • TAM – total global market if you owned 100 % share.
  • SAM – portion you can actually serve (e.g. AU metro households).
  • SOM – realistic 3-year share (e.g. 2 % of SAM).
  • CAC – cost to acquire one new customer (desktop search ads estimate).
  • CLV – lifetime gross margin from one customer.
