TAM – Total Addressable Market
“Size of the entire market if we owned 100 % share.”
Units: annual $ revenue OR total units per year.
Formula: customers × average spend.
SAM – Serviceable Addressable Market
“Portion our model can actually serve.”
Units: $ revenue or units/year in-scope.
Formula: TAM × percentage in scope.		 %
SOM – Serviceable Obtainable Market
“Realistic share we can capture in 3 years.”
Units: $ revenue or units/year we will win.
Formula: SAM × expected share (%).		 %
CAC – Customer Acquisition Cost
“Cost to acquire one paying customer.”
Units: $ / customer.

“Gross profit per customer over lifetime.”
